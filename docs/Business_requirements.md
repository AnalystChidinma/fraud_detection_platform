# Business Requirements

## Objective

Build a Fraud Analytics Platform that ingests transaction data, transforms it into trusted analytical datasets, and provides fraud insights through reporting dashboards.

## Business Requirements

### BR001

Calculate total transaction count.

### BR002

Calculate total transaction value.

### BR003

Calculate total fraudulent transaction count.

### BR004

Calculate total fraud amount.

### BR005

Calculate fraud rate.

### BR006

Analyze fraud by transaction type.

### BR007

Track fraud trends over time.

### BR008

Identify high-risk accounts.

### BR009

Measure fraud detection effectiveness using isFraud and isFlaggedFraud.

### BR010

Provide fraud insights through Power BI dashboards.
